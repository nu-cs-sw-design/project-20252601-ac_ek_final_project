# Cards Descriptions

## Alter The Future Card
- **Description**: Look at the top three or five cards of the draw pile, rearrange them in any order and put them back at the top of the pile.

## Attack Card
- **Description**: Force the next player to draw two cards. This can be stacked.

## Cat Card
- **Description**: A pair of matching Cat Cards allows you to steal a random card from another player.
- **Type**: Combo

## Catomic Bomb Card
- **Description**: Move all the exploding kittens to the top of the draw pile, and shuffle the remainder of the deck. You don't need to draw a card.

## Curse of the Cat Butt Card
- **Description**: Choose a player to curse. They must shuffle their hand, and are now unable to see their hand until they draw a card.

## Defuse Card
- **Description**: Defuse an Exploding Kitten. Place the Exploding Kitten back in the deck in any location.

## Exploding Kitten Card
- **Description**: Explodes when drawn. You must play a Defuse card or lose the game.

## Favor Card
- **Description**: Force another player to give you a card of their choice.

## Feral Cat Card
- **Description**: This is a Wild Cat Card (it can be any type of Cat Card). Play it with a Cat Card or another Feral Cat Card to steal a random card from another player.

## Garbage Collection Card
- **Description**: Every player must choose a card to shuffle into the draw pile.

## Imploding Kitten Card
- **Description**: When drawn for the first time, it must be placed back in the deck face up. When drawn for the second time, the player explodes even if they have a Defuse Card.

## Mark Card
- **Description**: Choose a random card from another player's hand. This card is now visible to all players, until it is played or stolen.

## Nope Card
- **Description**: Cancel any card that is played, except for an Exploding Kitten or a Defuse Card.

## Reverse Card
- **Description**: Reverse the order of play.

## See The Future Card
- **Description**: Look at the top three or five cards of the draw pile.

## Shuffle Card
- **Description**: Shuffle the draw pile.

## Skip Card
- **Description**: Skip your turn. If you have to draw two or more cards, you draw one less.

## Streaking Kitten Card
- **Description**: You can secretly hold one Exploding Kitten in your hand without blowing up. KEEP THIS A SECRET!!

## Super Skip Card
- **Description**: Skip your turn entirely, regardless of how many cards you have to draw.

## Swap Top and Bottom Card Card
- **Description**: Swap the top and bottom cards of the draw pile.

## Targeted Attack Card
- **Description**: Force a specific player to draw two cards. This can be stacked.
